Our programs


Education reduces poverty, We provide social impact loans for our talented scholars to attend schools. Educating a woman is the greatest social investment in the world today. Our programs are also geared toward the advancement of girls.


The Summer Food Service Program (SFSP) ensures that low-income children continue to receive nutritious meals when school is not in session. This summer, Watoto plans to serve more than 200 million free meals to children 18 years and under at approved SFSP sites.
